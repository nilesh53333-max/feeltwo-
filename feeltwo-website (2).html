Questions

Frequently asked.

Can any names be turned into a monogram? +
Yes — we work with any Indian or international names. Some letter combinations are more complex, but we've never had to refuse an order. Longer names may take slightly more time.
What files will I receive? +
You receive a clean SVG file (scalable to any size, perfect for tattoo artists and jewellers), an interactive HTML reveal file to share with your partner, and optionally a high-res PNG for printing.
Is it really hand-drawn, not AI? +
100% hand-drawn in Inkscape, stroke by stroke. We tried AI for a while — the results were unpredictable. Every design here is a human decision about geometry and proportion.
Can I use it for my tattoo / jewellery? +
Yes, that's exactly what it's made for. The SVG lines are clean and precise — any tattoo artist or jewellery engraver can work directly from the file. We recommend sharing the SVG, not a screenshot.
What if I'm not happy with the design? +
Couple Gift Package includes 2 revisions. For Digital Download, we offer 1 revision if the geometry doesn't feel right. We want you to love it.
How do I share the reveal file with my partner? +
Open the HTML file in Chrome on your phone, or we can host it for you at a private feeltwo.com/reveal/your-names link. Your partner taps each letter of your name to reveal it glowing in gold.
