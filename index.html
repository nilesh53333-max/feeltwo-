FAQ
Common Questions
Is FeelTwo really free? +
Yes — every user gets up to 50 free designs per day on WhatsApp. No credit card needed to try. If you need more designs in a single day, you can top up with credit packs starting at ₹49 (see Pricing).
How does it work? +
Open WhatsApp, message our number, choose a mode (paint, sofa, tiles, etc.), send a photo of your space. Our AI processes the image and sends back the result in seconds. You can try different colours or styles as many times as you want.
Do I need to download an app? +
No. FeelTwo works entirely inside WhatsApp. If you have WhatsApp on your phone, you have FeelTwo.
What kind of photos work best? +
Daylight photos with the full wall, room, or area visible work best. Avoid blurry, dark, or heavily zoomed photos. For exteriors, a clear daylight shot of your house. For walls, a straight-on photo of the room.
How accurate are the results? +
FeelTwo uses advanced AI to give realistic previews. Results are visualization aids — final paint or fabric colour may vary slightly based on lighting in your real room. Always confirm with a sample swatch before final purchase.
Are my photos private? +
Yes. Your photos are processed for AI generation and not shared. See our Privacy Policy for details on data handling.
What languages does FeelTwo support? +
FeelTwo supports English, Hindi, and Marathi for instructions and chat. Just message in any of these languages and the bot will understand.
